# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ISData Consulting is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone involved in this project—be it developers, designers, or users—will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior Includes:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Being constructive with feedback
- Focusing on collaboration over conflict
- Respecting diverse viewpoints and experiences
- Admitting mistakes gracefully
- Prioritizing what is best for the community

### Unacceptable Behavior Includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to race, gender, age, religion, disability, etc.
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Deliberate intimidation
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct considered unprofessional

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. They will take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident with specifics (date, time, people involved)
2. **Report** to the maintainers at: `conduct@isdataconsulting.com`
3. **Include** context and evidence if safe to do so

Maintainers will:
- Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- Investigate thoroughly and fairly
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters
- Take appropriate action

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Private warning or conversation
- Temporary or permanent ban from repository
- Removal from community spaces
- Legal action if applicable

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and forums
- Email communications
- Any official project spaces
